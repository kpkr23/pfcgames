Last Updated: January 2026  ·  PFC Games Studio

Overview

PFC Games ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our games and website, including Car Racing Robot Transform.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Your privacy is our priority.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • Gameplay data (scores, progress, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising ID for serving relevant ads
  • IP address and general location (country/region level only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve our games and services
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob / AdSense
  • To analyze gameplay and fix bugs
  • To maintain leaderboards and game progress
  • To respond to support requests

Advertising

Our games and website use Google AdMob and Google AdSense to display ads. These services may collect and use data to show personalized advertisements.

  • Google may use cookies and device identifiers to serve ads
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings
  • For more info visit: policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Game progress may be stored locally on your device or via Google Play Games Services. We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may include links or integrations with third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google AdMob (in-app advertising)
  • Google AdSense (website advertising)
  • Google Analytics (usage statistics)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent at any time
